Goodge Street Salon

A convenient hair salon 

Whether you need a stylish haircut or bridal hairstyling, Heena Beauty is your one-stop hair salon offering a variety of options. Based in Goodge Street, we are easily accessible from anywhere in West London. 

Men’s waxing and threading specialists 

In addition to providing full salon services to ladies, we also have trained professionals for men’s threading and waxing. Our experienced professionals have in-depth knowledge of everything from hair colouring to hair conditioning treatments. Our prices are highly competitive no matter what service you choose. Please check our prices at our Goodge Street branch.
