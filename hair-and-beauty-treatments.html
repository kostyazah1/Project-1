Quality threading and tinting 

Threading has become the preferred technique of hair removal for many people. It catches even the finest of hairs leaving you with clean lines and smooth skin. We undergo continuous training to keep up to date with the latest techniques and offer you the very best beauty treatments which include eyebrow tinting and eyelash tinting. 

We offer threading on: 
  • Eyebrow
  • Upper and lower lip
  • Chin
  • Side
  • Neck 
  • Forehead 
  • Full face 
  • Men’s eyebrows 

Our waxing services cover:
